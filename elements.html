Description

Arduino Mega 2560

The Arduino Mega 2560 is a microcontroller board based on the ATmega2560. It's the heart of our Smart Parking System, responsible for controlling various components and processing data.

Features

The Smart Parking System primarily utilizes IR sensors to detect the presence of vehicles in parking slots. This information is then displayed on the 16x2 LCD with I2C, allowing users to quickly identify available parking spaces. The dashboard shows if the parking slots are occupied with the help of the IR sensors present in the respective slots. When a vehicle enters the parking station, the IR sensor at the gate detects its presence and the barrier is lifted with the help of the servo motor if there are available slots in the station. Otherwise, the user is notified that all the spaces are in use, through the dashboard.
